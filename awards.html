How to Register

  1. Submit your entry form online.
  2. Your entry will be checked and vetted to ensure your nomination qualifies for the selected award/s.
  3. Successful applicants will be notified .

Rules for Entry

  1. All entrants must be legal citizens that serves the public or the industry.
  2. Your business is eligible to enter if it has been established and trading in its recent form for at least 12 months at the time of applying.
  3. Any entrants for New Business or New company awards will be eligible to enter if, at the time of applying, they opened within the past 18 months and were not included in the previous year's nominations.
  4. Previous year's winners and two runners up are automatically qualified in the following year's nomination for the same award.
  5. If your entry relates to a specific project, it must have been completed and fully implemented at the time of applying.
  6. LBIIW retains the right to disqualify entries.
  7. All Entry Forms must be completed in ENGLISH.
  8. Entries must reach LBIIW by the published closing dates.
  9. Only complete entries will be considered.
  10. Entries must be submitted online.
